# Novelty And Theory Gate

## Required Framing
- Gap statement: <specific unresolved gap in the literature or benchmark practice>
- Community importance: <why the target community should care>
- Theoretical mechanism: <mechanism, principle, or phenomenon being tested>
- Cross-field source: <math, science, or another field motivating the hypothesis>
- Falsifiable prediction: <prediction that can be checked by the planned experiments>
- Rejection condition: <result that would weaken or reject the theory>
- Contribution type: <theory|mechanism|principle|framework|phenomenon>
- Incremental-risk decision: <not-incremental|theory-driven|mechanism-driven>

## Verification Command
- `make verify-novelty`
